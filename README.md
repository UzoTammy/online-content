## 🎯 Project Vision

Build a data-driven content brand that captivates audiences through storytelling, not spreadsheets.

**Core Differentiator:** Extracting actionable insight from data in a way that separates us from 90% of content creators.

---

## 📊 Two Core Projects

### 1️⃣ SEASONAL: FIFA World Cup 2026 Intelligence
**Duration:** Pre-tournament + tournament (timely, algorithmic boost)

**Content Pillars:**
- **History:** Past tournament trends, GOAT debates, efficiency analysis
- **Coverage:** Live match breakdowns, tactical analysis, sentiment tracking
- **Prediction:** Simulations, probability models, upset forecasting

**Launch:** May-June 2026 (4-6 months before tournament)

---

### 2️⃣ PERMANENT: Head-to-Head Comparison Engine
**Duration:** Evergreen, repeatable, scalable

**Concept:** Pair famous entities (people, places, things) and compare across interesting, trending metrics.

**Examples:**
- People: Messi vs Ronaldo, Taylor Swift vs Beyoncé
- Places: New York vs London, Tokyo vs Singapore
- Things: Bitcoin vs Gold, iPhone vs Samsung

**Launch:** Immediate (easier to iterate, builds audience foundation)
